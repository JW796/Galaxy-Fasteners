Galaxy Fasteners offers a range of specialized services to meet your fastener needs. Our services include cut-downs, plating, re-threading, and patching. With our cut-down services, we can provide custom sizes to meet your exact specifications, ensuring that you get the perfect fit for your application. Our plating services include a variety of finishes to meet your specific requirements, including zinc, black oxide, and more. We also offer re-threading services to repair damaged threads or adjust thread sizes, saving you time and money. We also offer patching, an effective solution for damaged threads, allowing you to avoid costly replacements. With our range of specialized services, you can trust Galaxy Fasteners to provide the solutions you need to get the job done right.
