VMI


Our Vendor Managed Inventory (VMI) is a robust program that we will work with your company to customize to fit your unique manufacturing requirements. What can a VMI program do for your company:


  • Reduce lead times
  • Reduce price exposure risk
  • Improve overall manufacturing flow
  • Reduce overall cost

What do you need out of a VMI program? We offer a wide array of programs, from bin stocking to state-of-the-art hardware solutions such as Automated bins with real-time inventory. This allows us to anticipate your requirements before you have a chance to run out. We also offer RFID inventory management, to track your usage and keep inventory levels stable. These high-tech solutions are all customizable and easily modified to fit your requirements and existing processes.


The Goal


All these solutions are designed to keep your line up and running and work to eliminate downtime.
