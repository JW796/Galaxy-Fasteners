About the Company

Galaxy Fasteners is a premier fastener supplier based in Rhode Island, offering a specialized range of corrosion-resistant materials that includes 304/316 Stainless, Brass, Bronze, Aluminum, Monel, and other special materials. Our reputation for excellence is founded on the enduring customer relationships we have forged since our establishment in 1984. At our core, we place the customer first, and this is reflected in our commitment to delivering the highest quality products, competitive pricing, and exceptional service.
