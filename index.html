Who We Are

As a leading fastener supplier located in Rhode Island, we specialize in corrosion-resistant materials such as 304/316 Stainless, Brass, Bronze, Aluminum, Monel & other special materials. We attribute our reputation to the lasting customer relationships we’ve built since our establishment in 1984. Bin Stock

Our bin stock program is a comprehensive inventory management solution. We will work with you to set a schedule that fits your unique business needs and estimate production downtime. We can work with you to organize and install your bins for optimal operating efficiency.

VMI

Our Vendor Managed Inventory (VMI) is a robust program that we will work with your company to customize to fit your unique manufacturing requirements. Integrating a VMI program has the following impact on your business: reduce lead times, lower price fluctuation risk, and improve overall manufacturing flow.

JIT / Stock Release

The best way to reduce price volatility risk on hardware is with a stock release program. You place your annual quantity orders with us, and we will release to you, when needed. This helps to minimize inventory and manage one-time spending. We will work with you to develop a release strategy, whether that’s at pre-determined dates or when you run out!
